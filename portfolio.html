About Me

                        

                            I'm SanthoshKumar.S, a 2nd year Cybersecurity student at Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology. I am passionate about understanding how systems work and how to secure them against evolving threats. My journey in cybersecurity involves building strong fundamentals through academic coursework and hands-on practical experience.
                        

                        

                            I am actively exploring core cybersecurity domains including network security, ethical hacking, web application security, and cryptography. I am committed to gaining deeper technical knowledge and real-world experience, with the goal of contributing to a safer digital ecosystem.
